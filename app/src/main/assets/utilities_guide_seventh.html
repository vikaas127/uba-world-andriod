The major comparison sites estimate that you may need the following:
  • Browsing and email use: 2GB per month
  • Browsing and on-demand TV: 10-30GB per month
  • Browsing, on-demand TV, movies and music downloads: 40-80GB
  • Browsing, on-demand TV, movies and music downloads and gaming: 40GB to unlimited

If you think you’re likely to use 40GB and above, an unlimited option is probably best.


NEED FOR SPEED???

Be aware that low promotional pricing may be for the lowest speeds. Make sure the listed download and upload speeds are the ones that you're expecting, and if there's a trial period, test the service to see if it meets your expectations before you commit.

The fastest speeds are required if you are downloading more or less all the time and using applications that require immediate response, such as online gaming.


What’s a bundle deal?

A bundle deal wraps up your broadband with your landline and TV into one package.

To work out whether it is right for you you’ll need to compare the costs of what you pay for the separate parts with what you’d pay for the bundle.

The Internet is a very powerful machine! The good news is that you can search online, comparing quotes to find out which package is tailored for you.
